Our Mission

The Local Government Unit of Camaligan delivers responsive, transparent public service and manages resources to uplift the well-being of our people.

  • Service excellence and fast, efficient transactions.
  • Capacity-building and shared knowledge.
  • Program planning and rational use of funds.
  • Linkages with partners and stakeholders.

Our Vision

Camaligan, a significant heritage, settlement and tourism destination in the province—where empowered Camaligueños and a progressive economy thrive in a well-planned, ecologically balanced, and safe environment governed by honest and competent leaders.

Our Service Pledge

We commit to:

  • Create an enjoyable day-to-day transaction that will make the people comfortable.
  • Attend to all applications and requests within the premises of the office prior to lunch break and end of office hours.
  • Make sure our service exceeds expectations and performs our best to make service more reliable.
  • Always respect our clients and use their opinions as inspiration to continuously improve our services.
  • Initiate a high level of transparency and openness in providing accessible, accurate and reasonable information.
  • Guide the people in every transaction from start to finish.
  • Abide by the law and offer services fairly and responsibly without any bias.
